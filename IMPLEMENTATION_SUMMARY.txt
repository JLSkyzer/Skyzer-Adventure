                     QUEST ID NAMING CONVENTION
================================================================================

Chapters use prefixes:
- Chapter 1 (Welcome):        q01_ to q15_
- Chapter 2 (Tech T1):        t01_ to t12_
- Chapter 3 (Magic T1):       m01_ to m12_
- Chapter 4 (Combat):         c01_ to c14_
- Chapter 5 (Tech T2):        t21_ to t36_
- Chapter 6 (Magic T2):       m21_ to m34_
- Chapter 7 (Farming):        f01_ to f13_
- Chapter 8 (Synergy):        s01_ to s12_
- Chapter 9 (Space T1):       sp01_ to sp15_
- Chapter 10-16:              q01_ to q##_

UID Format:
- Chapter number (2 digits)
- Quest number (3 digits)
- Task/Reward number (2 digits)
Example: 05000121 = Chapter 5, Quest 12, Task/Reward 1
