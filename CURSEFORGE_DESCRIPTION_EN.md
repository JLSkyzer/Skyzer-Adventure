# Skyzer Adventure — Adventure Modpack (Minecraft 1.12.2) ✅

## Overview
Skyzer Adventure is a hand-crafted adventure modpack focused on progression and exploration. The pack offers guided progression through Tech, Magic, Space and endgame content, with dual paths (Tech vs Magic) that converge in late-game.

> Note: This modpack was private until a subscriber asked me to publish it.

---

## Highlights ✨
- Dual Tech & Magic progression lines
- Space exploration (Galacticraft, Extra Planets) and endgame content
- Cross-mod integration & balanced progression
- Multiplayer-friendly (team progression supported)

---

## Major mods included (sample)
- Thermal Series (Foundation / Expansion / Dynamics)
- Mekanism
- Applied Energistics 2 (AE2)
- Botania
- AstralSorcery
- Galacticraft + Extra Planets / More Planets
- Mystical Agriculture (+ Mystical Agradditions)
- SlashBlade
- Decocraft, Chisel, Macaw’s Building Blocks

Full list available in the `mods/` folder.

---

## Progression & Integration
- Integrated progression for Tech, Magic and Space content.
- Balanced reward tables and unlocks to guide player advancement.
- Documentation generated in `config/skyzer/generated/` and implementation summary files (see `config/skyzer/generated/`).

---

## Installation (Quick)
1. Use a launcher compatible with CurseForge / Overwolf. 
2. Install Minecraft 1.12.2 and a compatible Forge build. 
3. Copy/import the modpack files (or import on CurseForge). Ensure `mods/` and `config/` are present. 

Server: copy `mods/`, `config/` and world files; test locally first.

---

## Recommended settings
- JVM Memory: **6–10 GB** (depending on players & mods) 🔧
- For graphics issues, try adjusting video settings or disabling shaders.
